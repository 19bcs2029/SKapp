About us

Who we are...

SK Traders is a small fabric company located at Manpur Patwatoli, a small area of part of Gaya district in Bihar. It is almost 16km from the heritage site "Bodhgaya".

History of the Business...

Our locality mostly does the weaving of fabrics and so do we. It all started a very long time back. Only few people could own a powerloom and start the business that time. Now, it has come to modern era which uses modern powerlooms to manufacture the fabrics and the industry has grown to a large extent.

What we sell...

The company manufactures and sells a variety of fabrics. Some of them include Gamchha, Bedsheets, Rajai, Quilt-Covers, etc.
